About WTFDIVF

Why the fuck are you telling me how to vote?

Your vote is your choice. This site exists to help people find the candidate best-placed to challenge hard Brexit in the next Parliament. This is (for now at least) a free country, and if you don't like our recommendation you don't need to take it on board.

Where the fuck does this data come from?

We've looked at the results in each seat at the 2015 election (or more recently if there was a by-election) and recommended the party with the best chance of fighting hard Brexit in the next Parliament. Where a candidate campaigned for Leave, we have recommended the person with the best chance to replace them. Similarly, where a Conservative MP has been outspoken against Brexit, we have recommended them.
